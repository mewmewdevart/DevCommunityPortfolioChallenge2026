I worked as a **Web Designer** on the development of the landing page for the game **Hit It Back** for the studio **Sue The Real**, from **January 2025 to April 2025**.

- **Visual planning** of the project, including the creation of sketches and **simple wireframes in Figma**.
- **Interface development** using **React** and **Tailwind CSS**, ensuring a modern, responsive, and engaging experience.
- Implementation of a **newsletter subscription form** integrated with a **WordPress (PHP/MySQL) backend**.
- Application of **security best practices**, with a focus on preventing **XSS vulnerabilities**.
- **Version control and code management** using **Git and GitHub**.
- **Website deployment via HostGator** and direct communication with the studio through **Discord**.
- Prioritization of **accessibility (A11Y)** and **SEO**, ensuring inclusive navigation and improved search engine visibility.
